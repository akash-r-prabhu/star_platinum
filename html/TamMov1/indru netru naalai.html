INDRU NETRU NAALAI

Indru Netru Naalai is a 2015 Indian Tamil-language science fiction comedy film written and
directed by R. Ravikumar in his debut feature. The film is jointly produced by
Thirukumaran Entertainment and Studio Green, and features Vishnu Vishal and Miya
in the lead while Karunakaran, T. M. Karthik, P. Ravi Shankar and Jayaprakash play supporting roles.

Despite the boring first half, the film picks up speed in the second half. Credit should be given to the director for handling such a mind bending concept so simply and humorously.
The performances of the actors made their presence completely felt. The graphics isn't that advanced and could have been done it more effectively if they had afforded.
All in all, the film stands apart from the regular tamil movies.
