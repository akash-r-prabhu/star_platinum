21 JUMP STREET

21 Jump Street is a 2012 American buddy cop action comedy film directed by Phil Lord and Christopher Miller ,
written by Jonah Hill and Michael Bacall, and starring Hill and Channing Tatum.The film follows police officers Schmidt and Jenko,
who are forced to relive high school when they are assigned to go undercover as high school students to prevent the outbreak of a new synthetic drug
and arrest its supplier.
